Seeking Answers? We've Got You Covered!

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by May 1st, so we can have an accurate headcount. 😀

Can I bring a date?

Please check the RSVP for your +1! ❤

What time does the ceremony start?

The religious ceremony is scheduled around 3 PM, followed by the party/reception around 7 PM at Porto Del Sole Ballroom, Mamaia. Please check the Schedule section for any updates.

Will there be vegetarian/vegan options?

Yes, we've planned a diverse menu that includes options for various dietary preferences. Please let us know in advance if you have specific dietary needs.

Is there accommodation nearby?

Yes, there are several hotels and accommodations available in the vicinity. We've listed some recommendations on our Accomodation section for your convenience.

How do I get to the wedding venue?

If you're staying in Constanta/Mamaia, using a taxi service like Uber/Bolt is the best option. If you're arriving by car, there are parking spots available at the venue.

What's the dress code?

Attire for the wedding is cocktail/semi-formal. We recommend chic, semi-formal outfits with a touch of beachy flair to embrace the seaside charm.

Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?

Our wedding ceremony is outdoors, but our reception will be inside. 😎

Is it okay to take pictures with our phones and cameras during the wedding?

Yes! We would love for you to take photos and share them with us. 😍
